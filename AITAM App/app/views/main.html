About AITAM

AITAM

Ation Item Tracking And Management

AITAM is a collaborative tool to help associates to manage and track their action items to completion.
AITAM can be used to Create, Assign, Track, Review and Manage the Action Items of a team.
AITAM is developed using Yeoman, Ionic Framework, AngularJs, HTML5, CSS3, Sass, JS, NodeJs, Git, Chrome DevTools Google App Engine
